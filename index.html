Silksong

What is this game?

Silksong is a metroidvania developed by an austrian team of 3 called Team Cherry. You play as hornet who has been taken from her own land to the kingdom of Pharloom, a land haunted by silk and song. Her goal is to find out why she was brought to Pharloom by ascending to the shining citadel at the kingdoms peak.

Why do i like it so much?

I love this game because of how openly it is designed. In silksong by paying attention to your surrondings and understanding your own skillset you can completely break the "intended order" of progression. also i really enjoy the lore of Pharloom and how you will slowly come to learn about how Pharloom began to decline after uprisings and betrayals.

Limbus Company

What is this game?

Limbus company is a Korean RPG developed by Project Moon. you play as dante, an amnesiac with a clock for a head. you manage 12 "sinners" on a misson to collect the golden boughs. Each chapter focuses on a diffrent sinner and how the confront their past. the game is set in "the city" which is controlled by megacorporations and "the head".

Why do i like it so much?

I love this game for how its able to tackle the variety of issues that the sinners have. It tells amazing stories about war, grief, betrayal, love and life. despite the game featuring many supernatural elements it manages to keep its stories grounded in a way that allows us to connect to the sinners pain.

Tomodachi Life

What is this game?

Tomodachi Life is a game developed by nintendo where you create little characters based on your family, friends, media etc. these "Miis" go about their day to day life on an island where they can become friends, get into fights, get married and play games.

Why do i like it so much?

I get so much joy at watching the Miis do crazy things like multiple people confessing their love for someone or performing a song together. My sister managed to get our dad get married to a youtuber she liked, the stories that you can discover playing this game always leaves me laughing.

Stardew Valley

What is this game?

Stardew Valley is a farming sim developed by ConcernedApe. where you play as a new farmer in town. you can meet a variety of townfolk with their own stories to discover as you befriend them. You slowly build up your farm through the in game years as you become apart of the community

Why do i like it so much?

I find the game to be incredibly charming with its amazing pixel art. I also love the people in town who have very unique personalites with my personal favourites being Harvey the town's doctor and the wizard. I find the game to be very relaxing to play since I'm are never in a rush to do anything.
